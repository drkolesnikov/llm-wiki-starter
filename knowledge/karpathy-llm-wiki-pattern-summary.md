## Summary

The summarized pattern treats a Markdown wiki as a persistent knowledge workspace that compounds across agent and human sessions. Source material stays separate from synthesis. Useful queries can become durable pages. Agents help maintain the graph by adding notes, links, reviews, decisions, and lint results instead of leaving valuable reasoning only in chat.
